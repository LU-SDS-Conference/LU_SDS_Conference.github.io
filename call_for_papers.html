Call for Papers

International Conference on Cybernetics, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (CRAI 2027)

Conference Venue: Lingnan University, Tuen Mun, Hong Kong SAR, China

Conference Date: 7th ~ 9th May, 2027

Host: School of Data Science, Lingnan University

1. Conference Overview

The International Conference on Cybernetics, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (CRAI 2027) aims to build an interdisciplinary academic platform for researchers, engineers, and industry practitioners to exchange cutting-edge progress, share innovative findings, and bridge theoretical research and industrial deployment across cybernetics, robotics, and artificial intelligence. Benefiting from Lingnan University's global leading reputation in quality education and interdisciplinary research strengths in intelligent systems, CRAI 2027 gathers worldwide scholars to discuss core challenges, technical breakthroughs and future roadmaps of next-generation cyber-physical and intelligent systems. We welcome original and high-quality research papers, survey papers, and industrial application papers from academia and industry.

2. Topic Scope

CRAI 2027 accepts submissions including but not limited to the following three research topics:

Topic 1: Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning and deep learning, large language models, computer vision, natural language processing, multi-agent reinforcement learning, federated learning, privacy-preserving AI, trustworthy and explainable AI, intelligent decision-making

Topic 2: Robotics and Intelligent Systems

Robot perception and control, autonomous navigation, human-robot collaboration, swarm robotics, embodied intelligence, sensor fusion, motion planning, intelligent manufacturing, service and field robotics

Topic 3: Cybernetics and Control

Control theory and systems, feedback and adaptive control, cyber-physical systems, human-machine interaction, intelligent sensing and actuation, system identification, network control, biologically inspired cybernetics, complex system modeling and optimization

4. Submission Guidelines

  • Originality Requirement: All submitted manuscripts must be unpublished original work, without concurrent submission to other conferences or journals. Plagiarism and duplicate submission are strictly prohibited.
  • Paper Format: Manuscripts should follow standard ACM double-column format, with 6–10 pages for full papers and 3–5 pages for short papers, including figures, tables and references.
  • Review Policy: All submissions will undergo double-blind peer review organized by the conference program committee.
  • Publishing Index: Accepted papers will be published in CRAI 2027 conference proceedings, indexed by Ei Compendex and Scopus. Selected high-quality papers will be recommended to SCI/CCF indexed journals for extended publication.
  • Submission Channel: Electronic submission system will be opened on 1 August 2026 via the official conference website.
